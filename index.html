ABOUTS US 

Research Center for Agriculture Production is a small scale-based Production Center for learning and production platform for students and others. This has been developed with several units which the agriculture students can do practical and the same products can go for market so that the production Center
has its own identity in the research field. The production Center have the following units with the production capacities. This Center is also providing certificate and Skill courses in different units so that farmers or any entrepreneurs can be beneficial.


OBJECTIVE

The main objective of this research center is to utilize the ample facilities to produce paddy, mushroom, vermicompost , diary products , seed processing, Bio-fertilizers , Bio-Pesticides and also enhance the market opportunities to increase the wealth of the organization
